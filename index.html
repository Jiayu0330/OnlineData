An online source of data on my cause

The cause drives me to take this course is that I want to learn how website works and the ability to design web pages by myself, which has became more useful and necessary nowadays.
The data I found online is Young People Survey (2013) about their hobbies and interests. There were 32 items for them to choose how much they like each one. They could use 1 to represent "Not interested", and up to 5 to express "Very interested".
I counted how many times each integer appears and calculated the mean of each item. The data showed that young people were very interested in "Internet" (the mean is about 4.16). It reveals that the Internet has became more important in people's life, especially young people's life, and therefore supports my cause that learning about the Internet is a trend today. Young people who are interested in the Internet should learn how data and information are stored in the Internet and how to design your own web page to show something.
